Location Information (GPS)

Pocketdex: Monster Map Quest is a location-based game. To provide core gameplay features, we collect and use your device's GPS location data as follows:

  • Foreground location: Collected while the app is actively open and in use — used to display wild creatures, reward stops, and map features around your real-world position.
  • Background location: If you grant background location permission, we may collect your location while the app is running in the background — used to notify you of nearby rare creatures or active events without requiring the app to be open.
  • Location data is not sold to third parties.
  • Location data is used solely to deliver gameplay features and improve the in-game map experience.
  • You may revoke location permissions at any time via your device settings. Doing so will limit certain gameplay features.
